| 2026-06-09 | **Formal Telecom Council** — policy debate "Technological Sovereignty": ministers **welcome the tech sovereignty package**; broad convergence on reducing strategic dependencies across the digital value chain while preserving openness/competition, on avoiding vendor lock-in via public procurement and open source, and on auditability/replaceability of public-sector solutions. No CADA-specific positions, no formal step on this file. (Annotated agenda NL: Kamerstuk 21501-33) | [Consilium meeting page](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/meetings/tte/2026/06/09/) · [main results (IEU mirror)](https://ieu-monitoring.com/editorial/main-results-of-the-eu-telecommunications-council-on-9-june-2026/1243080) |

Committee responsible and lead rapporteur remain **"pending final decision on the referral"** — no formal referral yet. | [OEIL procedure file](https://oeil.europarl.europa.eu/oeil/en/procedure-file?reference=2026/0138(COD)) |

Substance not public; OEIL's Key-events table still shows only 03/06. | [EUR-Lex procedure history](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/HIS/?uri=CELEX:52026PC0502) |
